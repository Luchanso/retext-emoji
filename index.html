retext-emoji

Note: This demo contains unicode emoticons. Without proper rendering support, you may see question marks, boxes, or other symbols instead of emoticons.

A quick demo of retext-emoji. No cruft.

Just paste (or write) some text in the text area below, choose whether you want to encode (from “<3” and “:heart:” to ❤️) or decode (from “❤️” and “<3” to “:heart:”), and the converted output will magically appear next to it.

Please raise any issues on Github.

Note: For a list of supported emoticons, see wooorm/gemoji’s and wooorm/emoticon’s repositories.
