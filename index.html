retext-emoji

Note: This demo contains unicode emoticons. Without proper rendering support, you may see question marks, boxes, or other symbols instead of emoticons.

A quick demo of retext-emoji.

Just paste (or write) some text in the text area below, choose whether you want to encode (from “:cat:” to 🐱) or decode (from “🐱” to “:cat:”), and the converted output will magically appear next to it.

Please raise any issues on Github.

Note: For a list of supported emoticons, see Gemoji’s repository
